Our Story

Welcome to Delightful Bakers

Founded in 2015, Delightful Bakers started as a small family-owned bakery in Makindye, Kampala as the idea came from the great Molly whose love for pastry is unimaginable.The backery started in the home garage and only two people who had the backing knowledge worked in it,it has passed through different challenges which has exposed it to more experience and more opportunities to grow. Our passion for baking and commitment to quality quickly made us a local favorite.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, traditional recipes, and a whole lot of love to create baked goods that truly delight our customers.

From our signature bread to our decadent cakes, every item is crafted with care and attention to detail.
